Why

Everyone gets a brain and nobody gets the manual

Your brain decides how you sleep, what you remember, and how today feels. Most of us were never taught 1 useful thing about taking care of it. That's the whole reason this show exists. Each episode is a real conversation with someone who knows the science, and we stay on the question until the answer makes sense.

For the curious

You like knowing how things work, and this particular thing happens to be running your life.

For the caregiver

Someone you love has a changing brain. You want to understand what's happening and what actually helps.

For the professional

You work in healthcare and want the science kept straight, current, and worth your commute.

Why we make this show
We started Hello, Brain  because the science of caring for your brain was stuck in journals and conference rooms, and the people it could help were everywhere else. Our mission is to help millions of people build a better relationship with their brains.

Approachable, conversational, actionable. That's the whole editorial policy.

Every episode sends you home with something
